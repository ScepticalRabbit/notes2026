# Year in Industry Interview Screening Questions

1. **Pasion for Tech**: Tell me about a technical project that completely absorbed you. What was it about the problem that hooked you, and what was your specific contribution to solving it?


2. **Motivation**: In R&D, progress can be slow, and simulations or experiments fail frequently. What keeps you motivated when the breakthrough moments are months or even years apart?"


3. **Aptitude for Learning**: In our lab, you will constantly encounter concepts outside your current coursework. Tell me about a time you had to teach yourself a complex technical skill from scratch. [Follow up:] Based on that, if I asked you tomorrow to master [insert DIC/software], what are your first three steps?


4. **Critical Thinking**: What do you think are the key differences between simulations and experiments and how should we take this into account in high value engineering components?


5. Specific questions relating to candidates experience or CV
"What did you learn about yourself during X"
"What was the biggest technical hurdle in X"
